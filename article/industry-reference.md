# Industry Reference: Media, Sports, and Entertainment

This document explains how the demo maps to real-world Databricks use cases in media, sports, and entertainment.

## Key industry themes

- Ad tech campaign measurement
- Audience engagement and viewership duration
- Sponsorship ROI and revenue attribution
- Content monetization and cross-platform analytics
- Trusted reporting for partners and advertisers

## Demo relevance

The sample ad tech dataset is intentionally designed for digital media and entertainment scenarios. It includes:

- `campaigns` with cost and targeting metadata
- `impressions`, `clicks`, and `conversions`
- `viewership_duration` and engagement indicators
- business semantics such as CTR, viewability rate, ROAS, and average watch duration

## Why platform design matters

- Governance ensures that campaign definitions and metrics are consistent across teams.
- Data quality makes dashboards and AI outputs reliable.
- A semantic layer gives analysts and agents a shared language for business metrics.
- Lineage lets executives trust the numbers behind elegant dashboards and conversational insights.

## Thought leadership angle

This repo supports a broader narrative: the organizations that win in media and entertainment are the ones that treat the data platform as a product, not just an infrastructure project.
